New subscribers

This section covers FAQs for brides-to-be that are considering subscribing and need a little more details.

Miss To Mrs Bridal Box Subscription is a service that delivers themed boxes of trendiest bridal goodies & planning help throughout your wedding planning journey! Each Miss To Mrs Bridal Box has a different theme that was created to make a specific stage of wedding planning more exciting! Our team created uniquely themed boxes, filled with products you can use before your wedding day, on your wedding day and many even AFTER!

Your plan will be customized to your wedding date.

For example, if your wedding is 10 months away, you will get boxes monthly. However, if your wedding is 12+ months away we have options for every 2 months and every 3 months deliveries.

Your plan might also accelerate as you get closer to your date to ensure you get all the themes. So if you started with every 3 months, the plan will accelerate to every 2 months and then monthly closer to your date.

If you found us late and your wedding is less than 10 months away, your plan will adjust to either monthly, or we have options for accelerated bi-weekly deliveries.

If you need a custom plan, feel free to contact our 24/7 Customer Support team.

Yes, even if the wedding is just months away and everything is planned, there's still great value in subscribing to Miss To Mrs. We customize each subscription to cater to specific needs and wedding timelines, ensuring that the bride receives the most relevant box themes that align with her wedding planning journey. Besides, we offer accelerated delivery options such as bi-weekly shipments. If you're interested in even more frequent deliveries, you can always contact us at support@misstomrsbox.com and we'll arrange weekly shipments to suit your needs.
